Artist / illustrator, Wall Painting designer

About


I was born in November 17th, 1969, Daugavpils
In 1986 I was Graduated from Daugavpils Art Shool, and from 1987 to 1990 study in Latvian Academy of Art, Riga
I take my inspiretion from David Hockney, Mark Chagall, Quentin Blake, Nele Zirnite, Alexander Calder
My professional Membership: Daugavpils Regional Association of Artist
